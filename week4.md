# Week 4: Statistics

## Overview
Learn core statistics concepts. Estimated time: 7-10 hours.

## Learning Outcomes
- Calculate mean, median, distributions.
- Understand hypothesis testing basics.

## Lectures (5-7 hours)
- [Khan Academy – Statistics & Probability](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability)
- [StatQuest with Josh Starmer](https://www.youtube.com/user/joshstarmer)
- [MIT OCW – Probability & Statistics](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/res-6-012-introduction-to-probability-spring-2018/)

## Interactive Projects (2-3 hours)
- [freeCodeCamp – Data Analysis with Python](https://www.freecodecamp.org/learn/data-analysis-with-python/) - Mean-Variance Calculator.

## Resources
- [Stat Trek](https://stattrek.com/) - Tutorials.

## Homework Assessments
1. **Coding Task**: Calculate mean and standard deviation of a list in Python. Submit code.
2. **Quiz**: What is a normal distribution?
3. **Reflection**: How can biased data affect stats?
