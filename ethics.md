# Ethics in Data & AI

This guide covers responsible practices in data science and AI. Integrate these into your projects.

## Key Principles
- **Bias Awareness**: Data can reflect societal biases; check for fairness.
- **Privacy**: Protect user data; anonymize when possible.
- **Transparency**: Explain AI decisions.
- **Accountability**: Own your models' impacts.

## In Practice
- **Week 4**: Use fair sampling in stats.
- **LLM Weeks**: Avoid harmful prompts; test for bias.
- Tools: Use libraries like `fairlearn` for bias detection.
